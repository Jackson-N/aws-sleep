Jump Monke Jump!

AR Party Game (2022)

Made by Jackson Neal, Carson Strout, Sam Lummis, Chase Franklin, and Kevin Sherwood

'Jump Monke Jump!' is an AR party game made in Unity, where two players take turns going from one side of the course to the other, but with a twist. After every successful round for both players, each player gets to place an obstacle into the play-space. This allows for each gameplay experience to be different from the last.

My role on this project was with coding and art. I helped code some of the mechanics in the game for obstacles, and I did the art for all project assets. The theme that our team chose for this project were simple cartoon characters and objects. When designing this game, we took a lot of inspiration from party games such as Ultimate Chicken Horse and Mario Party.

We are the Hamster

2-D Interactive Comic (2023)

Made by Jackson Neal, Karla Watts, Steven Drain, Drew Fethke, and Gar Woodman

'We art the Hamster' is an Interactive Comic that talks about the ideas of death and how we as people think about death. The ideas that come from this project come from the book 'Biocentrism' by Robert Lanza. In this book he talks about how we as people and as generations of people share very similar experiences, and are in a way, more connected to each other than we think.

The comic takes place in an elementary school in which the class pet hamster dies. The professor and the kids have a talk about what it means to die and what happens after life. This topic was a very hard idea to tackle, since we as humans are going to never know what it is like after death, but we can make good guesses about what it may be like.

My title in this experience was as Team Lead. I helped to manage ideas between each member of the team, and help in both the art side of the project, and the coding of the game side. I helped to keep the members of the team feel like their voice was heard, keep ideas flowing during the early stages of the project, and to give us a clear direction for the project's success.

Dusk Till' Dawn

VR Horror Game (2023-Present)

Made by Jackson Neal

'Dusk Till' Dawn' is a VR Horror Experience that takes you into the boots of an unfortunate person stuck in the dead of night alone with their thoughts. The player must survive the night and use light sources wisely, or their insanity will get the best of them.

This game is my Senior Year Showcase game for when I graduate from BSU next year. Currently the game is a work-in-progress, but I am making constant changes to the game as development continues. Progress will be posted on my LinkedIn page and on GitHub.
